Meet Orah Russell

20+ Years of Experience

Compassionate Care for Every Senior

My name is Orah Russell. For more than 20 years, I've dedicated my career to caring for seniors—especially those navigating memory loss and dementia. As a Licensed Practical Nurse and Teepa Snow Certified dementia specialist, I understand what families face when aging becomes complex.

I'm not just a caregiver. I'm someone who listens, who shows up, and who treats every senior like they deserve dignity and respect. I believe in supporting not just the person in care, but the entire family carrying that responsibility.

Our Approach to Care

Compassionate, Specialized Care

Every senior deserves care that honors their dignity, respects their independence, and responds to their unique needs. I provide expert, evidence-based support grounded in Teepa Snow's dementia care philosophy.

Whether working with families navigating early signs of memory loss or providing intensive support for advanced dementia, my goal is always the same: to create a safe, respectful environment where seniors thrive and families have peace of mind.

I don't just deliver care. I become a trusted partner in your family's journey.

Caregiver Training & Education

Empower Yourself to Care with Confidence

Caring for someone with dementia requires specialized knowledge and compassion. I offer personalized training based on Teepa Snow's evidence-based dementia care approach—helping family caregivers and care professionals understand behavioral changes, master communication strategies, and provide person-centered care.

Gain the confidence, skills, and knowledge to deliver expert, dignified care for your loved one. Whether you're a family member stepping into the caregiver role or a professional seeking advanced training, I provide guidance tailored to your needs and experience level.
